Our Roots

The Voice of the Ancient Earth

Drewno is more than a musical group; it is a vessel for the resonant power of "white singing" (śpiewokrzyk) – a traditional vocal technique from Central and Eastern Europe characterized by its raw, open-throated intensity.

Our ensemble gathers the forgotten fragments of rural life, transcribing polyphonic melodies from elderly masters and the rustle of the wind through the pines. Each performance is an invitation to witness the living archive of our collective ancestry.

"Voices that bridge the gap between stone and sky."
